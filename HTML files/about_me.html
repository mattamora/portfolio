About Me

Currently, I’m a senior at California State University, Fullerton, majoring in Computer Science and set to graduate in May 2025. My technical expertise includes C++, Python, SQL, and various front-end technologies, and I’m always eager to expand my knowledge and improve my skills. Known for being a quick learner, I thrive on challenges and embrace opportunities to grow both personally and professionally.

Music is another passion of mine—I love playing the guitar and exploring different kinds of music. Whether I’m debugging a complex problem or refining my three-point shot, I approach every task with focus and determination. I’m excited to channel my creativity and technical skills into building impactful and user-friendly web experiences.
